The Best Anime According to Me

My Top 3 Anime of All Time

Attack On Titan

My top 1 best anime because it has the best plot twist ever. The anime I was thrilled the most. I love the characters even though some of them got killed in the end. No more words to say. It's just the best.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Best animation and adaptation ever. Love the main characters and looking forward to watching the latest season.

Demon Slayer

Top tier animation but the won't beat jujutsu. I love the plot twist and the story and also the character development among the characters.
